General information

  1. Hover your mouse over a square on the grid to change its color.
  2. Click the button to select a color.
  3. Click the "Clear Grid" button to clear the color from the grid.

Alex's Etch-a-sketch

Changing the size of the grid

  1. Click the "Number of squares" button.
  2. Type the number of squares to create in the grid.
    • Note: The value you enter applies to both the number of squares in a row and column.
